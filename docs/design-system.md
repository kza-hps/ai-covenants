# aiCOVENANTS Design System

## Brand Position

aiCOVENANTS is a human-first public covenant ledger for ethical AI use in creative and commercial work.

It records how AI helped create a work, what human labour may have been displaced or deferred, and what pledge or covenant the creator makes in return.

The platform should feel like a public archive, civic trust record, philosophical journal, and restrained AI-era product.

It must not feel like crypto, SaaS, legal tech, Patreon, or an AI content marketplace.

## Brand Essence

- Trust-first, not hype-first
- Quiet authority
- Human dignity in the age of AI
- A covenant, not just a contract
- Public record, not private dashboard
- Ceremony with utility
- Serious, elegant, and quietly firm

## Logo Meaning

The logo combines a tablet and chisel.

This represents digital craft joined with ancient making.

The lowercase `ai` signals AI as the lesser partner.

The muted gold on charcoal signals dignity, restraint, and weight.

The Hero’s Journey Creative mark may appear only as a small, unobtrusive studio/copyright mark.

## Palette

### Obsidian Charcoal

`#232A2F`

Primary dark background. Used for hero sections, ceremonial panels, and high-weight surfaces.

### Stone Gold

`#D6C39A`

Primary accent. Used for logo, dividers, status details, ceremonial buttons, and fine borders.

### Slate Mist

`#596066`

Secondary neutral. Used for subdued text, borders, panels, and supporting metadata.

### Soft Ivory

`#F2EBDD`

Primary light background and text colour on dark surfaces.

## Typography

### Display / Wordmark

Elegant classical serif, high contrast, lightly tracked.

Suggested direction:

- Cinzel
- Cormorant Garamond

Use for:

- page titles
- covenant titles
- ceremonial headings
- public record headings

### Body / UI

Clean modern sans-serif.

Suggested direction:

- Inter
- Manrope

Use for:

- forms
- navigation
- body copy
- labels
- metadata
- buttons

## Layout Principles

- Generous spacing
- Low-noise layouts
- Strong vertical rhythm
- Thin dividing lines
- Subtle borders
- Clear public-record hierarchy
- Avoid clutter
- Avoid dashboard density
- Avoid decorative excess

## UI Mood

The interface should feel like:

- a public archive
- a civic trust record
- a philosophical journal
- a ledger of serious promises
- a restrained AI-era product

The interface should not feel like:

- crypto
- web3
- legal tech
- Patreon
- generic SaaS
- AI content marketplace
- social media
- startup hype page

## Core Screens

Initial design should cover only:

1. Landing page / manifesto page
2. Founding covenant catalogue
3. Covenant detail page

## Founding Catalogue

The MVP catalogue contains:

1. VouchMeApp
2. FASCIA
3. Ho and the Baby Eater
4. aiCOVENANTS
5. ElevenLabs personal voice-use covenant request

Do not create fake covenant examples.

## Covenant Detail Structure

A covenant detail page should include:

- Work
- Creator
- Status
- AI-use declaration
- Human labour displaced or deferred
- Acknowledgements
- Beneficiaries
- Value-sharing intent
- Version history
- Withdrawal status, if applicable

## Covenant Capture Start

The first covenant flow should feel ceremonial but practical.

Initial steps:

1. Name the work
2. Declare the AI contribution
3. Record the covenant or future pledge

Primary calls to action:

- Start a Covenant
- View Example

## Visual Restrictions

Avoid:

- neon colours
- electric AI gradients
- crypto/web3 patterns
- cartoon icons
- glossy SaaS cards
- excessive rounded corners
- fake legal seals
- fake blockchain motifs
- overused AI sparkles
- hype copy
